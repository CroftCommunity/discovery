Durable brand/identity assets for Croft (the name center of gravity — see `../NAMING.md`).

| File | What it is | Notes |

| `croft-wordmark-pastoral.png` | "CROFT" wordmark with a fuller pastoral crofting scene — grass, wildflowers, a winding stream and footpath, lily pads, stones — inside the letterforms; dark-green outline on a near-white ground. | 1024×559 PNG, ~674 KB. Both are **draft explorations, not a settled final mark**. They embody the crofting metaphor (tended
smallholding, local-first, "grow your own") directly in the type. Two directions on the same idea:
`pastoral` is the fuller landscape (with water); `wildflower-kraft` is the drier, paper-handmade take.
Pair with `thinking/app/brand-and-voice-notes.md` (voice) and `NAMING.md` (why "Croft").

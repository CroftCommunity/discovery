## 2026-07-14, Corroboration dials and the beam contract (design pass)

Scope: a markdown-only design pass, no code, landing the owner's framing of what final state costs a node that can never see past its own edge. Two `Design` paragraphs into Part 2, one backlog item, one new thinking note. The framing was owner-decided and not re-opened.

**The quantified-trust framing.** §5.1 is restated as the epistemic floor: everything a local authority holds about the outside world is comparative or asserted, never canonical, so all corroboration is quantified trust and the node's job is not to *know* the edge but to be configured with the terms under which corroboration counts as sufficient. Sufficiency is a setting (the k of §7.4, and the act classes it gates), not a discovery a node can make about a global state it has no vantage on.

**The stamp-as-beacon reading.** The pass turned on the read/write asymmetry. On write a node *does* know the last event it asserted on top of, because every outgoing entry references it; the §7.4.3 governance-generation stamp makes that legible, so every ordinary data-plane message doubles as a passive currency beacon (the "I'm good, you?"), with R6 attributable acceptance (§7.5.1) as its enforcement-side twin. This closes the behind-via-traffic case (an ahead-stamped entry names a sized, known gap the reader fills before enforcing) and leaves exactly the unreferenced tail. The read side is solicitation: a frontier ask reaching the tail no stamp can announce, whose answer is always an assertion at quantified trust, so an empty solicitation is corroborated absence, never established absence.

**The two Part 2 `Design` paragraphs.** §7.3.3 gained the corroboration-dials paragraph after the load-bearing-caveat paragraph: the residual of completeness-ahead once the stamp closes the behind-via-traffic case is a family of R7-governed settings (which act classes require final state; the freshness k or its formula; the solicitation posture), safe at every setting because the fail-closed rule (delay over breach) is not a dial. §7.4.1 gained a final paragraph admitting a formula-valued freshness threshold: a Group MAY set k as a formula over folded state (proportional to folded member count, or role-weighted) provided every input is folded fact, so k stays deterministic and identical everywhere; it moves the dial, not the machinery. DR language (conventions A.11) applied: continuity-framed, non-moral. Details in `part-2-changelog.md`.

**The self-echo exploration (with its two seams).** A node's own stamp echoed back in others' entries is delivery corroboration without receipts, kept exploratory in the new note and *not* promoted to spec, held to two hard seams: positive-evidence-only (no echo means nothing, since silence and partition are indistinguishable) and liveness-signal-only (the moment an echo touches ordering or authority it is a covert clock, which Part 1 §2.0.1 forbids). For anyone else an echo may mean nothing, which is consistent with corroboration being quantified trust.

**The new thinking note.** `alpha/thinking/corroboration-and-quantified-trust.md`, companion to `reconciliation-horizon.md` and `the-shape-of-disagreement.md`: the epistemic floor, the already-built write side, the read side and the unreferenced tail, formula-valued thresholds, the self-echo exploration, and the beam reframed as a dial family plus one intrinsic honest limit (earning the beam means demonstrating the contract, not eliminating the limit).

**EXP-C1 (backlog §2c).** The completeness-ahead contract, loopback-runnable now with no new infrastructure, four RED-able assertions: stall-at-threshold (delay over breach, reads continue), stamp detection (the behind-via-traffic case end to end), solicitation reach (the unreferenced tail surfaced by a frontier ask and folded identically to normal arrival), and formula-valued k (ceil(n/2) over the folded member set, identical across arrival orders). Shares boundary machinery with EXP-H1; discharges at loopback grade part of §8.2(e)'s residual that the freshness precondition on originating such an op is not yet exercised over live transport.

Spec effect: two Part 2 `Design` paragraphs (§7.3.3, §7.4.1) and two §0 map lines; no mechanism or code change, no posture-table change. The demonstration contract is carried as backlog EXP-C1, unrun.
